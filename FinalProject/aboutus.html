About Us


The Philippine Mythological Creatures and the Unknown is a comprehensive online accessible encyclopedia that serves as a treasure trove of knowledge, delving into the captivating realm of mythical creatures originating from the Philippines and the encounters with the unknown. It presents a wealth of information and fascinating narratives about a diverse array of mythical beings, providing readers with an immersive experience.

Each mystical being is accompanied by vivid and captivating visual depictions that authentically capture their unique and awe-inspiring appearances. These visuals offer a glimpse into the enchanting and often mysterious realm from which these creatures emerge. Other than mere aesthetics, this encyclopedia offers intricate details about the abilities and behaviors of these mythical creatures. From the powerful and fearsome to the gentle and benevolent, each creature's characteristics are explored in depth, shedding light on their powers, origins, and their place within Philippine folklore.
