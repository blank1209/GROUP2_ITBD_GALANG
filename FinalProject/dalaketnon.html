Dalaketnon

Abilities:
  • Telekinesis
  • Duplication

Dalaketnons are a race of elf-like creatures in Philippine mythology. In Visayas, they were thought to be handsome and beautiful creatures that resembled nobles and monarchs of prehispanic Philippines. They live on Dalakit trees (Balete, Dakit), thus the name Dalakitnon, which literally means "from the Dalakit or Dakit tree."

Story:
They dress stylishly, live in "haunted house-like" mansions, and try to blend in with mortals. Some believe that entering the Dalaket trees is the only way to get to Dalaket, their home. These beings kidnap people and transport them to their world. They feed their victims and force them to consume the Black Rice, which casts their spell and turns them into slaves.
