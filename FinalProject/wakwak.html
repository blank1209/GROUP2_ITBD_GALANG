Wakwak

Abilities:
  • Have wings as sharp as a knife
  • Long sharp talons

In Philippine legend, a bird-like vampire creature called the Wakwak exists. Similar to the Manananggal and the Ekek in rural Philippines, it is rumored to kidnap humans at night as prey.

Story:
The sound that a tuko (common house gecko) makes at night when out of sight was discovered by an American who spent much time in the Philippines to be attributed to a Wakwak. Other than that, many bisaya people or people living in southern part of the Philippines believed that the sound "Wak-Wak" is just a sound from a bird which only flies at night and gave terrifying sounds to anyone nearby. Wakwak prowls the Barrio during the day in search of a potential victim. They transform into a little bird that resembles a crow. Even the bird's feet and beak were black. They would be able to tell if there were any sick or dying people if they did this. They are also out looking for a body. These are the preparations they make so that they can simply take the victim later on in the evening.
